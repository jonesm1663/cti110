The next step after you begin collecting is deciding how to protect your books
Bags and boards are the first step in protecting your comics. The boards keep your
bending. The bags keep the books dry and protect it from dirt.

Learn

  • What kind of bags and boards to get? There are a lot different bags and boards brands out
    there. They come in three basic sizes golden age, silver age, and current. like most things with
    comics you have to try a few to see which one you like the most.
  • How To use them? First insert the boards into the bags. Then put the comic in between cover
    side up. Tape the back and you are done!
  • Where to find them? All comic book stores carry bags and boards. Some stores even include a
    free set with purchase. Mostly bags and boards are sold in sets of 100.
