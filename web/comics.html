What Comics to Collect? The simple answer is what ever interest you at the time.
Don't worry so much about the value when choosing what to read. Remember
comic collecting is about having fun!


  • What Comics to Read? The main thing in collecting is to have to fun collecting.
    Try as many comics as you can! I promise you will find have fun!!
  • Where to Find Comic?You can find comics at several different places. Most cities have at least one comic shop.
    Major chain stores like Barns & Nobles are starting to carry more comics all the time
  • Are Conventions worth it? Conventions are a great place to find back issues of books.
    You can fill in wholes in your collection or find rare comics.
